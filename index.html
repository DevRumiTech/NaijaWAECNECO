WAEC vs NECO Grading Explained

Both WAEC and NECO use grades from A1 (best) to F9. Although the examination bodies are different, the grading scale is the same and widely accepted across Nigeria.

What Your Grades Mean

Your grades help institutions assess your academic readiness. Strong results increase your chances for university admission, scholarships, and competitive opportunities.
